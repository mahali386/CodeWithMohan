Who am I?

Hello! I'm Mohan Mahali, a passionate and hardworking web developer from Odisha, India. I have a deep interest in web technologies and love building interactive, fast, and responsive websites that provide real value to users. Currently, I’m pursuing my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Mechanical Engineering from Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology (VSSUT), Burla. Even though my academic background is in engineering, my true passion lies in the world of web development and programming.

Over time, I’ve developed strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design. I enjoy turning ideas into beautiful and functional websites with clean code and smooth user experiences. I'm also familiar with version control systems like Git and platforms like GitHub. I believe in continuous learning and always stay curious about new technologies and tools that can enhance my development skills. My goal is to become a full-stack developer and contribute to real-world projects that make a difference.
